What is an Akan name?

In Africa and especially West african countries, newborns are given special names based on their gender and the Corresponding day of the Week. Diverse ethnicities have variants and meanings as a symbol of cultural integrity and ancestral affiliation. Below is sampled names for Ghanians;

Female AkanName and Corresponding Weekday.

  • Sunday - Akosua
  • Monday - Adwoa
  • Tuesday - Abenaa
  • Wednessday - Akua
  • Thursday - Yaa
  • Friday - Afua
  • Saturday - Amma

Male AkanName and Corresponding Weekday.

  • Sunday - Kwame
  • Monday - Kwasi
  • Tuesday - Kwadwo
  • Wednessday -Kwabena
  • Thursday - Kwaku
  • Friday - Yaw
  • Saturday - Kofi
